Marmite surprise

Description

Marmite is a wonderful yeasty spread that feels great when stuck to the roof of a person/dog's mouth

You should probably return to the index now

Ingredients

  • Jar of marmite
  • piece of bread

Steps

  1. Open the jar of marmite and place bread on the bench (no need for hygiene here).
  2. lather marmite onto the piece of bread untuil there is more marmite on it than bread.
  3. Take the Marmite + bread demonfood and creep up behind an unsuspecting victim
  4. force their mouth open and stick the bread to the roof of their mouth
  5. run
